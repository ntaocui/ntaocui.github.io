As part of a final course assignment, I was assigned to a team of four to create a functional robot that could be used to help solve any self-chosen issue. We decided to create a robot that could help one train different aspects of table tennis. My role in the team was to help program the robot as well as design and create its main components. Weather Advisor

The Weather Advisor is a browser based app made with Javascript, HTML and CSS. I made this app on my own during my first hackathon. It uses a weather API to take in the inputed location's climate in JSON and then proceeds by recommended an assortment of articles to bring outside with you. I was inspired to create this app as I myself always tend to wear too much or too little when leaving my university dorm. I figured if I had this issue, many other people must as well.

Bot or Not

At the end of summer, my friend and I joined together to create a multiplayer game inspired by the eccentric personality of CleverBot. Once all players join the server, they are prompted to converse with one another in order to figure out who is the CleverBot among them.
Table Tennis Trainer
In the MTE100 course that I had taken for my program, students were given the responsibility to create any robot of their choosing. My group ended up deciding on creating a robot that could help someone train in table tennis. My own responsibilities within the group included designing and integrating the loading system as well as developing sturdy code for the robot. Created with LegoNXT, 3-D printed and laser-cut parts (with a hint of cardboard), the robot was a huge success!
Weather Advisor
This project was inspired by my inability to correctly gauge the weather by looking out the window of my university dormitory. This web application suggests to the user what kind of clothes to wear depending on the temperature outside. Though the code itself is not very complicated, I am proud to present this as my very first hackathon project.
